# Pixel-fidelity review

Compare at the recorded viewport and device class. Inspect major geometry,
grid, alignment, spacing, sizes, typography, color, radii, elevation, icons,
navigation, content density, visible state, RTL, and interaction affordances.
Check every major region rather than overall attractiveness. Record platform
rendering tolerance separately from layout drift. Reject undocumented changes
to hierarchy, navigation, state, or approved component appearance.
