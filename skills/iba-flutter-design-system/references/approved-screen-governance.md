# Approved-screen governance

`approved-binding` images control implementation composition. Supporting
references explain patterns but never replace a binding screen. Archived
references are non-authoritative. Resolve IDs through the manifest, open the
image, preserve its bytes, and link it in tasks, reviews, deviations, and status
updates. New/superseding designs require approval, provenance, versioning, and
preservation of the prior original. An agent cannot self-approve a deviation.
